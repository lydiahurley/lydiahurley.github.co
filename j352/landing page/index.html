Scentsations - College Park, MD

Scentsations is College Park's only hub for high end, ethically sourced, small-batch candles and other home furnishings! Not only are the candles made locally in Prince George's County, but they are made in small batches depending on the freshest herbs and oils we have picked that season. We offer in-store and online shopping for our candles + home furnishings. Our staff also honors special requests to fit any and all scentsational needs!
